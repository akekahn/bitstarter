Gameply

The core gameplay experience lies in the tactical battles of the hero-team against opposed teams.

Players can create any hero they wish to and put him (and his team) to the trial in a big player-versus-player tournament / fast matches.

Avatar Mechanics

Primary Attributes

Peoples avatars have these primary attributes. They are gained through training or special quests and events. Players do not see numeric values, only a textual description (for example: Weak, Strong, Herculean). Because continuous training is necessary to keep the attributes on a high level, it is impossible to get all the attributes to the maximum level.

Avatar Training

Every day, the Character gains a certain amount of training units/points, to a certain max (which can be reached in ~3 days). Those training units can then be spent to increase the primary attributes. (But since the primary attributes are going to be unlearned over time, they continuously need to be invested).

Strength

Strength determines a character's physical power. The more muscle you have, the more pain you can inflict with your melee attacks.

Dexterity

Dexterity determines a character's nimbleness and agility. It helps to evade enemy attacks and increases the accuracy of ranged fighters.

Intelligence

Intelligence increases a character's brain power. It helps memorizing spells and strengthens magical attacks.

Wisdom

Wisdom increases the magical defense and healing powers.

Charisma

Charisma influences the ability to lead other characters. With more charisma, more and stronger peons can be used in battle.
